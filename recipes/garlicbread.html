Garlic Bread time!

Fresh garlic bread like you've never before. Scott Pilgrim approved!

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup/ 8 tablespoons unsalted butter (1 stick) melted
  • 4 fat garlic cloves, grated
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan
  • 1/4 cup firmly packed chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Go ahead and eyeball some black pepper in there
  • 1 French baguette or rustic crusty loaf
  • 1/2 pound freash mozzarella, thinly sliced (optional)
